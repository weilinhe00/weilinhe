About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Bristol, supervised by Cristina David and Meng Wang. My passion for research is fuelled by my conviction that I am engaged in meaningful work with the potential to make a real impact on society.

Research Interests

  • Neuro-Symbolic Reasoning Combining LLMs' generative power with symbolic logic for verifiable code synthesis.
  • Correctness-Guaranteed GenAI Developing frameworks to ensure behavioral consistency in automatically generated code.
  • Early Error Detection Leveraging formal verification for incomplete programs to improve generation efficiency.
  • Scalable Modular Generation Managing complex module dependencies and dependency graphs in large-scale codebases.
